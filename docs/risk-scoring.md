# Risk Scoring

```txt
risk_score =
  0.35 * task_volume_index +
  0.20 * overtime_hours_index +
  0.20 * deadline_pressure_index +
  0.15 * sentiment_stress_index +
  0.10 * leave_pattern_index
```

## Intervention policy
- Medium risk: weekly check-in by HR.
- High risk: immediate workload redistribution.
- Critical risk trend (2+ high cycles): manager + HR escalation.
